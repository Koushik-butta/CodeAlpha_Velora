Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Velora free to use?
Yes! Registering and listing products on Velora is completely free. You only pay when you purchase a product through the platform.
How does the exchange feature work?
You can request to exchange your product with another user's product. If both parties agree, the exchange is confirmed and you swap items directly. No cash required!
Is my payment secure?
Absolutely. We support Cash on Delivery for all orders. Your personal data is encrypted and never shared with third parties.
How do I verify my seller account?
Complete your profile with your phone number and address, and build your Trade Score by completing successful transactions. Verified badges are awarded automatically.
Can I list products from any city in India?
Yes! Velora is available across India. Buyers can filter listings by city to find deals near them.
